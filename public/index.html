The AVoided Emissions and geneRation Tool (AVERT) is a free tool designed to meet the needs of state air quality planners, energy officials, public utility commission staff, environmental agency staff, professionals in the clean energy field, people working on climate mitigation or climate action plans, and other interested stakeholders. AVERT can be used to evaluate how energy efficiency (EE) and renewable energy (RE) policies and programs displace emissions from electric power plants at a county, state, or regional level. View more information about AVERT and how it works.
